At TLI, we believe that educated, employed, and engaged young people possess the power to solve the world’s toughest problems.

For 30 years, we’ve led and sustained coordinated action to harness the talent and potential of the world’s young people, now numbering 1.2 billion. At the heart of our efforts is building partnerships, initiatives, and curricula that prepare young adults to succeed as citizens, employees, entrepreneurs, and change-makers.
